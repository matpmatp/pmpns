🎫 How to Get Your Student Card
1. Get your official study confirmation letter from STU student office (study dept., 1st floor)
2. Go to any DPB (Dopravný podnik Bratislava) office — nearest is at Hlavná stanica (main train station)
3. Bring: passport, confirmation letter, 1 passport photo, €3.50 fee
4. Card valid for the semester — keep it with you on every journey!
