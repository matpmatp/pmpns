The Reality

Exchange life is exciting.
The admin isn't.

🗣️
Language Barriers
Locals don't always speak English. Official documents, dormitory forms, and transit instructions are all in a language you don't understand.
"Go to the designated location. Get documents while barely communicating."
📋
Administrative Overload
From housing contracts to bank setup, phone plans, and health insurance — the mental load of arriving in a new country is immense.
"For an Erasmus you need over 20 papers to handle."
🏠
Dormitory Chaos
Students arrive without knowing what to expect in their accommodation, what paperwork is needed, or what conditions await them.
"I would have liked specific advice about the dormitories."
🍜
Finding Affordable Food
Google Maps 4.5 stars means something completely different in each country. Students waste hours searching for budget-friendly student spots.
"I usually do it by looking through tens of Google Maps results."
🗓️
Scattered Information
Important dates, events, and university announcements are spread across emails, portals, WhatsApp groups, and social media.
"Put all university communication in one place and translate it."
🚌
Navigating Transit
Local public transport systems require local apps, local language, and local knowledge — none of which a new student has on day one.
"How to use public transport and get a student discount."
What Roamly Does

Everything you need,
one place

01
📄
AI Document Translation & Form Assistant
Upload any official document and Roamly translates it accurately, explains confusing legal terms, extracts your personal details, and guides you through filling it out — step by step. No more "translate and pray."
✓ Dormitory forms · Housing contracts · University docs

02
📅
Smart Unified Calendar
Roamly scans your university emails and portal, extracts all important dates — registration deadlines, orientation days, cultural events — and builds you one clean calendar automatically.
✓ Never miss a deadline again
03
🍽️
Curated Student Food Finder
Filter by budget, distance, and student recommendations — not generic star ratings that differ country by country. Find places other exchange students actually love.
✓ Budget-aware · Student-reviewed
04
🎉
Local Events & Social Discovery
Find calm cultural gatherings, city tours, and student meetups — not just parties. Filter by vibe and add events directly to your schedule with one tap.
✓ ESN events · Cultural tours · Quiet socials
05
🚌
Transit Guide with Student Discounts
Step-by-step local transit directions explained in your language, including how to get student discount cards for public transport in your city.
✓ Explained in plain language

Open the app on arrival and get a personalized checklist of everything you need to do in the first 24 hours — sorted by urgency, translated, and clearly explained.
✓ Personalized to your university & city
