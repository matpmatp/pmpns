Student Discount Guide
💳 How to get a student pass
1. Go to any DPB office (Mon–Fri 8–16)
2. Bring student ID + passport
3. Ask for "Mesačné predplatné — Študent"
4. Monthly pass = €13.20 (vs €24 regular)
🚉 Key Lines for STU Students
Bus 32, 39 — Mlynská dolina area
Tram 4 — City center to campus
Bus 93 — Night bus (runs till 2AM)
⚠️ Always validate your ticket when boarding. Inspectors are frequent — fines are €50.
